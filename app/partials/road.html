GROUP RIDES

Frisco Cycling Club regularly attends group rides around the Frisco community. Additionally, FCC leads several weekly training rides. See details below.

For the latest info on planned rides, visit us on FACEBOOK.
Day Start Time Meet Location Details
Tuesdays 6:30 PM Superdrome Parking Lot We ride out to Craig Ranch area and do laps around Stacy Rd and Alma. With 6 laps it ends up being 33 miles. Pace is in the low 20's, but the loop allows for frequent re-group opportunities
Thursdays 6:30 PM Coffee and Cream parking lot (Legacy and Eldorado) This ride heads north on Legacy -> Panther Creek -> Teel and eventually heads out to the Tribute golf club. The route is just over 30 miles with a pace ranging from 21 - 23+. This is an advanced ride requiring paceline skills.
Saturdays 7:30 or 8:00 AM Richardson Bikemart Frisco This ride heads north up to Celina through Westin. The route is approximately 65 miles with a pace between 18 - 20. There are limited regrouping opportunities on this ride.
