Our Club

The Frisco Cycling Club is dedicated to advancing the sport of cycling with riders of all levels through local events, group rides, races, and community involvement.

GOALS

The FCC aims to build community involvement, community awareness, physical education and participation through club activities and charitable events. We dedicated to enabling the sport of cycling within local and regional communities by hosting events, offering clinics, and teaming up with charities. FCC regularly partners with organizations to raise money contributing to local community assistance programs, as well as national level medical research and development fund raisers.

SPONSORSHIP

The Frisco Cycling Club has obtained new sponsors for 2012 and have continued the relationship into the 2013 season. Richardson Bike Mart remains a long time premier sponsor of FCC, as does Crest Auto Group as a second year premier sponsor of the club. The club is registered with USA Cycling under the name “Crest/RBM Cycling”. Please visit our sponsor's tab for more information about all the club's sponsors.

RIDERS

FCC is comprised of various types bicycling enthusiasts. The core of FCC consists of men, women, and children interested in recreational cycling. There is a small racing contingency that was founded primarily on track racing at the Frisco Superdrome, and has recently expanded to road and criterium racing. Mountain biking has also become a core discipline of FCC, with rider participating in racing as well as endurance challenges. For more information please refer to tabs specific to your riding interests.
